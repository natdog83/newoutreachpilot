    Outreach Pilot

    JDRF’s Outreach Program is the primary entry point into the organization, presenting programs and opportunities to the T1D community who will join us in our work to accelerate life-changing breakthroughs to cure, prevent and treat T1D and its complications. With the help of trained and experienced volunteers, JDRF staff seeks to meet individuals with T1D and their families in their time of need order to build lasting relationships which will ultimately lead to their loyalty to JDRF’s mission. JDRF Outreach strategies have been evaluated and opportunities for growth were identified to better support chapters and branches in implementing an effective Outreach Program. The JDRF Mentor Program is recognized as an area with great opportunity to strengthen the approach to nurture relationships with those new to JDRF.
